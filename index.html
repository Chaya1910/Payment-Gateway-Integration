Our Mission

"Through this initiative our attempt is to feed the millions of children in India who lack the means, but have the zeal to learn and achieve. By feeding them and providing education we we give them nourishment and the motivation they need to pursure an education for better future. It is our endeavour to reach out to every child at the grass root level of society."

About Us

The Sparks Foundation is an organization based in India based in Goa. Established in 1998, we have worked extensively for more than two decades to reach out and help thousands of children from the underprivileged segment. The Sparks Foundation works with 45 branches focusing on providing food and education to the needy kids. We do this through well-planned and comprehensive programs in health and education making us the best charity organization in India.
